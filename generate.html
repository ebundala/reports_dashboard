info About This Report

The Accounts Receivable report shows current balances for customers. It is designed to help you track outstanding payments effectively.

  • check_circle Can be generated as of a selected date to show historical balances.
  • check_circle Includes both posted and unposted payments for comprehensive view.
  • check_circle Only includes bills that are posted.
  • check_circle Does not include deposit related charges or payments.
